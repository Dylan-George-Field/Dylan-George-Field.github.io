About

I'm here to inspire you to get on a bike and see the world.

I grew up in Sale, Victoria, where my dad taught us how to ride on the farm (below). We rode trails while camping as a kid. I got my road license when I was 18.

Touring the USA by motorbike was my dream. I did it in 2018. It took me 5 years to get there and I came back with memories that will last a lifetime.

If you enjoy my content, feel free to leave a comment or a donation.
