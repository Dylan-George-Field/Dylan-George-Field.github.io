About

Mission

I want to inspire you to see the world.

To feel the wind through your fingers and the earth between your toes. You'll discover incredible people, their stories and struggles. Find exciting places and explore the land in-between. Face your fears, challenge yourself and you will become a better person.

All you need is a motorbike.

Dylan

My dad taught me how to ride a motorbike on a farm in, Sale, Victoria (pictured below). We rode trails on weekends while camping as a kid. I got my road license when I was 18 and have ridden ever since.

Media

I love to work with others and promote adventure riding. If you need a story, inspirational speaker or help planning your next adventure, contact me.
